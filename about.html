About Us

Our Journey in Industrial Innovation

Incorporated in the year 2000, at Tiruppur, (Tamil Nadu, India), “Vizzac Laser” are one of the prominent manufacturers and suppliers of a wide range of industrial machines and equipment such as Wood Engraving Machine (CNC Router), Roll Fusing Machine and Paper Cup Forming Machine. These products feature specifications as per their operational requirements and provide a high efficiency and productivity to their industrial processes. We are widely recognized in the industry for providing our clients a range of industrial machines which are designed and fabricated after extensive research of the requirements of clients. Thus, these products are manufactured using the most advanced technology available in the industry to enable optimum functionality and efficiency. Our quality assurance further helps us ensure a high productivity and functionality of these products before these are delivered to clients. Catering to numerous industries such as sculpture, shoes, electronic components, advertisement, gifts, leather textiles and stamps, we are widely acclaimed as one of the leading organizations in this domain.
