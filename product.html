CUTTING MACHINE

Laser Engraving and Cutting Machine (Normal)

A laser engraving and cutting machine uses a focused laser beam to cut, engrave, or mark materials. "Normal" refers to standard CO₂ or fiber laser machines.

  • CO₂ laser suitable for wood, acrylic, leather, paper, plastics.
  • Some machines use fiber lasers for metals.
  • Power usually ranges from 40W to 100W.

Small Camera Vision Laser Cutting Machine

Integrates a high-resolution camera with a CO₂ laser cutter for precise, automatic contour cutting.

  • Efficient cutting of printed or embroidered fabrics.
  • Precise cutting of logos and designs for signs.
  • Laser power: 40W–150W for small/medium models.

High Speed Top Camera Laser Cutting Machine

Combines advanced camera systems with high-speed laser technology for precise and efficient cutting of intricate patterns.

  • Efficient cutting for garments, banners, textiles.
  • Precise cutting of logos and designs.
  • Power ranges from 60W to 300W.

Flying Laser Cutting Machine

High-speed laser system designed for continuous material processing. Moves laser head across material at high speeds.

  • Laser moves rapidly without stopping.
  • High-resolution cameras detect patterns for contour cutting.
  • Power usually ranges from 60W to 200W.

MARKING MACHINE

Fiber Laser Marking Machine

A fiber laser marking machine uses a high-power fiber laser to permanently mark or engrave metals and some plastics with high precision. Ideal for serial numbers, logos, and detailed designs.

  • Marks metals like stainless steel, aluminum, copper, and brass.
  • Can also mark plastics and coated materials.
  • Laser power typically ranges from 10W to 50W for standard models.

UV Laser Marking Machine

A UV laser marking machine uses ultraviolet laser light to create precise, high-contrast marks on materials that are sensitive to heat. It is ideal for plastics, glass, and delicate materials

  • Marks plastics, glass, ceramics, and thin materials without heat damage.
  • High precision for detailed graphics, QR codes, and serial numbers.
  • Laser power typically ranges from 3W to 20W for standard models.

Sheet Metal & Tube Cutting Machine

The Sheet Metal & Tube Cutting Machine is engineered for dual-purpose operations, allowing precision cutting of both flat metal sheets and round or square tubes. It offers unmatched flexibility and accuracy for industrial fabrication.

  • Efficiently cuts sheet metals like stainless steel, carbon steel, and aluminum, as well as metal tubes of various shapes.
  • High-speed laser technology ensures smooth, burr-free edges and consistent results.

Enclosed Metal Sheet Cutting Machine.

The Enclosed Metal Sheet Cutting Machine is built with a fully covered design to ensure safe, clean, and efficient cutting operations. It provides high precision and protection against laser radiation and dust.

  • Fully enclosed design ensures operator safety and maintains a clean working environment.
  • Ideal for cutting stainless steel, carbon steel, aluminum, and other metal sheets.
  • High-performance laser power options ranging from 1kW to 12kW for industrial-grade cutting.

Laser Welding Machine

The Laser Welding Machine delivers precise, high-strength welds with minimal distortion. It’s ideal for industries requiring accuracy, speed, and seamless metal joining.

  • Suitable for stainless steel, carbon steel, aluminum, and other metals.

Laser Cleaning Machine

The Laser Cleaning Machine provides an eco-friendly and efficient solution for removing rust, paint, oil, and contaminants from metal surfaces without damaging the base material.
