Vizzac Laser: Powering India’s Industrial Growth

Precision machinery that drives productivity and innovation across industries.

In today’s fast-moving industrial world, success depends on one thing — precision with productivity. Whether it’s cutting metals, engraving designs, or printing on fabrics, businesses now rely on high-tech machinery that delivers both speed and accuracy. One company that has built its reputation on this foundation is Vizzac Laser, a leading manufacturer and trader of industrial laser and CNC machines based in Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu.

For over 15 years, Vizzac Laser has been a trusted name for small, medium, and large enterprises seeking reliable, high-performance machinery. With a 4.5-star rating and a growing customer base, the company continues to make a strong mark in the Indian manufacturing market through its wide range of products and transparent service approach.

Diverse Product Portfolio

  • Fiber Laser Metal Cutting Machine – ₹23,50,000; 3000×1500 mm working area; cuts up to 20 mm metal sheets with 0.03 mm accuracy.

  • Chain Link Fencing Machine – ₹9,50,000; efficient chain link fence production.

Other offerings include CNC Routers, Die Making Machines, Wood Carving Machines, Air Plasma Cutting Machines, and Roll Forming Machines, serving local artisans to industrial production units.
